Mammoth Steak

"This succulent mammoth steak is seasoned with fragrant juniper berries, sweet honey, and savory spices, then roasted to perfection. Juicy and flavorful, it's a delicious way to enjoy this hearty and versatile meat."

Ingredients

  • 1 large Mammoth Steak
  • 1 tbsp Juniper Berries
  • 2 sprigs Fresh Rosemary
  • 1 clove Garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix together crushed juniper berries, rosemary, garlic, honey, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Brush the mixture onto the mammoth steak, making sure to coat it evenly on all sides.
  4. Place the steak on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F for medium rare.
  5. Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
