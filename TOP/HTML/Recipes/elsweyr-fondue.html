Elsweyr Fondue

This creamy and indulgent fondue is made with Elsweyr cheese, heavy cream, and fragrant spices, then spiked with a generous splash of Cyrodillic Brandy. Perfect for dipping bread, apples, and vegetables, it's a decadent treat that's sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 1 wheel Elsweyr Cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 cup Cyrodillic Brandy
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Moon Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine grated Elsweyr cheese, heavy cream, garlic, moon sugar, cumin, Cyrodillic Brandy, salt, and pepper. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until the cheese has melted.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and lemon juice. Add this mixture to the pot and stir well.
  3. Let the fondue simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened to your liking.
  4. Serve with bread, sliced apples, and vegetables for dipping.
