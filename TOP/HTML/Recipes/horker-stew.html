Horker Stew

This hearty and satisfying stew features tender chunks of horker meat, simmered with potatoes, carrots, and onions in a rich beef broth and ale base. Warm and comforting, it's the perfect dish for a chilly evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Horker Meat
  • 4 cups Beef Broth
  • 2 cups Diced Carrots
  • 2 cups Diced Potatoes
  • 1 cup Ale
  • 1 whole Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, and cook until onions are translucent.
  2. Add horker meat and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add carrots, potatoes, beef broth, and ale to the pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and some broth from the pot. Add this mixture to the pot and stir well.
  5. Let simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the stew has thickened to your liking.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
